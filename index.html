Who We Are

Our Story

"وَاذْكُرُوا اللَّهَ كَثِيرًا لَّعَلَّكُمْ تُفْلِحُونَ"

"And remember Allah often that you may succeed." — Al-Anfal 8:45

Al Maqam Al Mahmoud was born from a deep love of the Prophetic tradition and a longing to revive the art of devotional nasheeds in the hearts of our communities. We are a group of brothers united by love of the Prophet, peace and blessings be upon him, and a sincere desire to bring beauty and remembrance into every gathering we serve.

Our name — The Praised Station — is drawn from the Quran, a reference to the supreme intercession granted to the Beloved of Allah. Every nasheed we perform is an act of love, a vessel of dhikr, and an invitation for listeners to draw closer to their Creator.

We perform at masajid, community dinners, mawlid gatherings, Islamic weddings, fundraisers, Eid celebrations, and educational events. Our repertoire spans classical Arabic nasheeds and original compositions — all a cappella, all from the heart.

الإمام ياسين محمود

Imam Yaseen Mahmoud — Quran Teacher

Imam Yaseen offers private and group Quran lessons rooted in the classical tradition — covering proper recitation, the rules of Tajweed, and guided memorisation for students at every level, from complete beginners to those wishing to deepen and refine their recitation.

Lessons are conducted with patience, clarity, and a deep love for the Book of Allah — suitable for all ages, in sha Allah.
